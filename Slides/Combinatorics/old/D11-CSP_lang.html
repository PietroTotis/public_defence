1. Multisets - Constraint Modelling Languages

Constraint modelling languages have better support for sets, but multiset are still no first-class citizens.
Essence[1] is a constraint language designed to specify combinatorial problems and includes types for multisets, but limited.
Multiset type: letting M be mset(B,A,N,A,N,A) ✓
find s : sequence (size 6) of M ✕
Unnamed types: letting ns be new type of size 2 → ns={n1,n2}
  1. A.M.Frisch, W.Harvey, C.Jefferson, B.Martínez-Hernández, and I.Miguel, "Essence: A constraint language for specifying combinatorial problems", Constraints, vol. 13, no. 3, pp. 268–306, 2008
