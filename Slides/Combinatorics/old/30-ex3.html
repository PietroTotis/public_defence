When variables are exchangeable we can propagate constraints.
$\#\mathit{defective}\land \mathit{purchase} \geq 2$: propositionalize to cases $=2$ and $=3$
$P_1$ propagates $\#\mathit{defective}\land \mathit{purchase} = 2$: Variables: $V=\langle tv_1,tv_2,tv_3,tv_4,tv_5\rangle$
Domains: $D=\langle DFs,DFs,\lnot DFs,\lnot DFs,\lnot DFs\rangle$
$P_2$ propagates $\#\mathit{defective}\land \mathit{purchase} = 3$: Variables: $V=\langle tv_1,tv_2,tv_3,tv_4,tv_5\rangle$
Domains: $D=\langle DFs,DFs,DFs,\lnot DFs,\lnot DFs\rangle$
