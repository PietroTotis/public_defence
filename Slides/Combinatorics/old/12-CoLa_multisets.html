1. Multisets - CoLa: Combinatorics Language

In CoLa there are two ways to declare multisets.
  1. Enumeration of the objects
    universe letters = {a,a,a,n,n,b}
  2. Declaration of the number of copies
    property a; property n; property b;
    #a=3; #n=2; #b=1;
Properties are subsets of the multiset of objects.
Two objects are indistinguishable iff. they have the same properties.
