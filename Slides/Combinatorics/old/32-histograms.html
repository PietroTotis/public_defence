Otherwise splitting relies on relevant sets and histograms.
Relevant sets maintain the coarsest (most lifted) universe partition
If we are concerned about defective TVs only defective is relevant and not its subsets, e.g. "broken screen", "faulty cable",...
Histograms count the elements of each relevant set.
