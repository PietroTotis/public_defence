2. Splitting

Splitting is the of dividing a problem $P$ into two independent subproblems $P_1$, $P_2$ such that the multiplication rule is applicable.
A typical split of a problem $P$ is to consider a (random) variable $v$ and a value $c$ from its domain.
Let $P_1$ be $P$ plus the constraint $v=c$.
Let $P_2$ be $P$ with the constraint $v\neq c$:
$P_1$ and $P_2$ are a split for $P$.
