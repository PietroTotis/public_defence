3. Propositionalization

Lifted reasoning is not always possible, in such cases traditional reasoning on the propositional level (propositionalization) is necessary.
Propositionalization is useful to define subproblems where lifted reasoning is again possible.
"at least two defective TVs" has no lifted level rule. Propositionalization of the admitted values, i.e. 2, 3 (4 and 5) allows us to do lifted reasoning on two subproblems.
$$\binom{3}{2} \cdot \binom{9}{3} $$
$$+$$
$$\binom{3}{3} \cdot \binom{9}{2} $$
