Research question 1A
What are the key characteristics of a modelling language for encoding combinatorics math problems?
Example

            labelled property tvs;
            property defective;
            #tvs = 12;
            #(tvs & defective) = 3;
            purchase in { tvs };
            #purchase = 5;
            #(purchase & defective) >= 2;
