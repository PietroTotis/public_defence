Inhibition effect

Negation in the head: express the inhibition effect on a logical atom.
                
                    0.4::rain. 0.7::wind. 0.6::remember.
                    umbrella :- rain, remembers, not wind.
↓ 

                0.3::friend_brings_umbrella.
                ¬umbrella :- friend_brings_umbrella.
Without inhibition: $P(umbrella\,|\, remember, rain, \lnot wind) = 1$
With inhibition: $P(umbrella\,|\, remember, rain, \lnot wind) = 0.7$
