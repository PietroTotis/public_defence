2A. Modelling Beliefs

Research question 2A
Can probabilistic logic programming model probabilistic argumentation problems?
Example

                0.6::bias(ht_X). 
                0.8::bias(ht_Y).
                0.4::bias(exp). 
                0.2::bias(noise).
                arg(X):-bias(X).
                0.7::¬arg(ht_Y):- arg(ht_X).
                0.4::¬arg(ht_X):- arg(ht_Y).
                0.8::¬arg(ht_Y):- arg(noise).
                0.9::¬arg(ht_X):- arg(exp).
                query(arg(X)).
Contribution 2A
A novel mapping from probabilistic argumentation problems to PLP programs based on rules with negated head (inhibition eﬀect).
