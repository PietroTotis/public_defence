Conclusion

Declarative modelling and reasoning for:
  1. Combinatorics math problem solving: CoLa and CoSo.
    1. CoLa provides the modelling primitives to encode the fundamental concepts.
    2. CoSo provides the automated reasoning tools to efficiently solve the problems.
  2. Probabilistic argumentation: smProbLog.
    1. Its semantics support modelling beliefs by means of the inhibition effect.
    2. It provides the automated reasoning tools for probabilistic inference and learning under the new semantics.
